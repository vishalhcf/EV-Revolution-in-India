# Electric Vehicle Revolution in India 🚗⚡

This repository contains our team research project on the rapid growth of Electric Vehicles (EVs) in India.

## 📌 Overview
The project provides a detailed analysis of the EV ecosystem by examining both:
- Consumer perspective (awareness, adoption, challenges)
- Government perspective (policies, subsidies, and initiatives)

## 🎯 Objectives
- To understand the current state of EV adoption in India  
- To analyze consumer behavior and preferences  
- To study government policies and their impact  
- To identify key challenges and opportunities in the EV sector  

## 📊 Key Insights
- Increasing consumer interest in EVs due to rising fuel prices  
- Government incentives playing a major role in adoption  
- Infrastructure (charging stations) still needs improvement  
- Growing market trends and future potential  

## 🛠️ Technologies / Tools Used
- Data Analysis  
- Research Methodology  
- Charts & Visualization  

## 📁 Project Structure
- `/data` – Dataset used in research  
- `/analysis` – Data analysis and findings  
- `/report` – Final research paper/document  

## 🚀 Conclusion
The EV revolution in India is accelerating, driven by both policy support and changing consumer preferences. However, challenges such as infrastructure and cost barriers still need to be addressed.
